🍽️ Top Foods To Try (Ordered)

  1. Ugali & Isombe (Cassava leaves)
  2. Brochettes (Goat or beef skewers)
  3. Akabenz (Fried pork)
  4. Ibihaza (Pumpkin with beans)
  5. Fresh Rwandan Coffee
