🍜 Nyamirambo

The cultural heartbeat of Kigali — vibrant streets, local food, art, and music.

✨ Things to Do

  • Nyamirambo Women's Center walking tour
  • Try brochettes and fried chicken at Mambo Mambo
  • Visit the Green Mosque (Masjid Al-Fatah)
  • Nightlife at Club Rafiki
  • Explore local art galleries
  • Visit local fabric markets

🍽️ Places to Eat (Ordered)

  1. Meze Fresh - Mediterranean & local fusion
  2. Repub Lounge - Live music + Rwandan dishes
  3. Cafe Neo - Great coffee spot
  4. Ubumwe Restaurant - Traditional cuisine
